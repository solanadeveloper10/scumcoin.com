Who is $HEADR?

In the deep jungle lies a creature known as $HEADR. It hunts for the finest meal, with its favorite being brains. $HEADR slurs its tongue deep into heads and skulls to feast on the juicy brain meat. Now, it is once again on the hunt for the finest meal. Legend has it that the lower the IQ of a person, the juicier and more delicious it becomes. Let the hunt begin!

It is the first artwork ever showcased on Matt Furie's website, http://www.mattfurie.com, $HEADR is a mythical creature with infinite meme potential and is remarked as the most proud and intrinsic work from Matt Furie himself.
